Coleoptera

Beetles

Insect order Coleoptera is comprised of beetles.
Beetles are categorized by their front wings, which have evolved into a hard shell (elytra) covering the hind wings when at rest.

Common Traits of Beetles

  • Elytra
  • Wings
  • Hard exoskeleton
  • Many uses of camoflauge
  • Holometabolous
  • Mandibles
  • One pair of legs on thorax, other two on abdomen

Etemology

  • Named by Aristotle
  • The name Coleoptera is based off of their elytras
  • Coleoptera comes from the greek "Koleopteros"
  • Koleos - Sheath
  • Pteron - wing
  • The name Beetle comes from Old English.
  • "Bitela" - little biter
  • "Bitan" - to bite
  • Middle English "Betylle"
