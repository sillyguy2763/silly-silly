Coleoptera

beetles!

Insect order Coleoptera is comprised of beetles. Beetles are categorized by their elytras covering their front pair of wings.

Common Traits of Beetles

  • Elytra
  • Wings
  • Hard exoskeleton
  • Many uses of camoflauge
  • Holometabolous
  • Mandibles
  • One pair of legs on thorax, other two on abdomen
