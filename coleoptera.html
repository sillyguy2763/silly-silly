Coleoptera

Beetles

Insect order Coleoptera is comprised of beetles.
Beetles are categorized by their front wings, which have evolved into a hard shell covering the hind wings when at rest.

Common Traits of Beetles

  • Elytra
  • Wings
  • Hard exoskeleton
  • Many uses of camoflauge
  • Holometabolous
  • Mandibles
  • One pair of legs on thorax, other two on abdomen
